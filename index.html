How I work
I find the
path forward.

Every production has a moment where the plan meets reality. Budget pressure, a timeline shift, a stakeholder pivot. I do not escalate. I navigate. The work still ships. The work is still good.

That is the value I bring beyond the Emmy shelf.

01
Clarity before chaos
I align creative intent with business objectives at the brief stage so mid production surprises are rare, not routine.
02
Systems not just instincts
I build workflows, Workfront structures, and team rituals that make great work repeatable and not dependent on a heroic sprint.
03
Lean when it needs to be
I have run six figure productions and shoestring shoots. I know how to protect quality when the budget does not cooperate.
04
The room trusts me
C suite, vendors, editors, athletes. I speak every language on a production. That fluency is what keeps things moving.
